Lights, camera, action!: In our Movie Making Camp, young filmmakers will create an original short film—from writing the script and designing costumes to acting on camera. Campers will learn every step of the moviemaking process, including storyboarding, filming, and adding music and effects. Our instructors handle the technical side while students take the spotlight.
